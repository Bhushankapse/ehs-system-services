About EHS

"EHS (Environmental Health and Safety) is the practice of ensuring human health and environmental protection by managing risks and promoting safety measures. It encompasses various areas such as occupational safety, industrial hygiene, environmental management, and emergency preparedness. EHS professionals work to prevent accidents, injuries, and environmental damage while ensuring compliance with relevant regulations."
